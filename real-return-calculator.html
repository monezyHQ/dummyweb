ðŸ’¡ Equity Tax Insights (India)

  • This calculator estimates tax based *only* on the capital gains entered.
  • **STCG (Short-Term Capital Gains):** Taxed at a flat rate of 15% (plus applicable cess/surcharge) under Section 111A.
  • **LTCG (Long-Term Capital Gains):** Taxed at 10% (plus cess/surcharge) under Section 112A, but only on the gain exceeding â‚¹1 lakh in a financial year. Gains up to â‚¹1 lakh are tax-free.
  • **Loss Set-off (Not Calculated Here):** Tax rules allow setting off losses against gains (STCL vs STCG/LTCG, LTCL vs LTCG only) and carrying forward losses for up to 8 years. This calculator does *not* perform loss set-off adjustments. The calculated tax assumes the gains entered are net gains after any applicable intra-head set-offs for the year.
  • Indexation benefit is **not** available for LTCG on listed equity shares or equity mutual funds.
  • Dividends received are added to your total income and taxed separately at your slab rate.

ðŸ’¡ Mutual Fund Insights (India - Assuming Equity Funds)

  • Mutual Funds pool money from investors to invest in stocks, bonds, etc., offering diversification.
  • **SIP (Systematic Investment Plan):** Invest a fixed amount regularly, benefiting from rupee cost averaging.
  • **Lumpsum:** Invest a large amount at once.
  • **Expense Ratio:** An annual fee charged by the fund house, directly reducing your returns. Lower is generally better.
  • **Taxation (Equity Funds - Assumed Here):** Similar to direct equity. STCG (< 1 year) taxed at 15%. LTCG (> 1 year) taxed at 10% on gains exceeding â‚¹1 lakh per financial year.
  • **Taxation (Debt Funds):** Different rules apply (taxed at slab rate if bought after Apr 1, 2023). This calculator uses Equity fund rules.
  • Capital loss rules similar to Equity apply (set-off/carry-forward not calculated here).
  • Returns are not guaranteed and depend on market performance. CAGR for SIP is complex and displayed as '-' here.
